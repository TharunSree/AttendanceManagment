For Administrators
  • Managing Courses & Subjects: Navigate to Admin Management > Manage Courses to add or edit courses and their subjects by semester.
  • Managing Classes: Go to Manage Classes to create new class groups (e.g., "BCA Section A") and assign them to a course.
  • Bulk User Import: Use the Bulk Import feature in User Management to quickly add multiple students or faculty from a CSV file.
  • Marking Schemes: Define how marks are calculated by creating criteria (e.g., "Internal Exam", "Assignment") under Marking Schemes.
For Faculty
  • Daily Schedule: Your dashboard shows your daily schedule, including regular, substitution, and extra classes.
  • Marking Attendance: Click on a class from your schedule to mark attendance. You can also edit recent attendance records from the "Edit Past Attendance" page.
  • Scheduling Extra Classes: Use the "Schedule Extra Class" tool to create additional classes. An announcement will be automatically sent to the students.
  • Entering Marks: Go to "Enter Marks" to input student scores based on the active marking scheme for their course.
For Students
  • My Timetable: View your weekly class schedule and any extra classes scheduled for the current day.
  • My Attendance: Check your subject-wise and overall attendance percentage for the current semester.
  • My Marks: View your marks for each subject, broken down by the criteria set by the administrator.
